Rules of Tag Questions

Structure:-

  1. Auxiliary verb থাকলে বসবে। না থাকলে, Do / Does / Did বসবে।
  2. Negative Sentence হলে not হবে না। কিন্তু, যদি Affirmative sentence হয় তাহলে- Aux verb + n't হবে।
    Note: not এর বদলে সর্বদা n't হবে।
  3. Subject এর pronoun + ?

STR: Auxiliary verb + n't(if needed) + pronoun + ?

Negative Words

no, not, nor, never, neither, few, little, very few, scarcely, saldom, hardly, no sooner etc.

Short Forms:-

notn't
am notain't, aren't
shall notshan't
will notwon't
can notcan't
Examples
  1. He hardly works, does he?
  2. Rahim will come here tommorow, won't he?
  3. Everybody wants to get A+, don't they?
