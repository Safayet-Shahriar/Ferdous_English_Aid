Completing Sentence

1st Conditional Sentence.

str: If/Unless + Present tense + Sub + will/may/can + V1 + ext.

Examples:
  1. Unless you study regularly, you fail in the exam.
  2. If you are not united, you will fall in danger.
  3. Unless he utilizes his time, he will face many problems.

2nd Conditional Sentence.

str:

If / unless + Sub + V2 + ext Sub + would/could/might + V1 + ext
were + Sub + ext
had + Sub + ext
Examples:
  1. Were I a bird, I could fly in the sky.
  2. Had I the wings of bird, I could fly.
  3. Unless he studied, he would fail in the sky.
  4. Unless Apon knew English, he could not get a good job.

3rd Conditional Sentence.

str:

If/Unless + Sub + had + V3 + ext. Sub + would/could/might + have + V3 + ext
Had + Sub + V3
Examples:
  1. Had he been a king, he could have helped the poor.
  2. If he had been a little careful, he could have avoided Corona-Virus.

4. Lest(তা নাহলে).

str: ...Lest + Sub + should/might + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. Take umbrella lest, you might dranch.
  2. Read attentively lest, you might fail in the exam.

5. As if/ As though(মনে হয় যেন).

str:

Present Sub + V2 +ext
Sub + were + ext
Past Sub + had + V3 + ext
Examples:
  1. He talks as if, he knew everything.
  2. He pretended as though, he had known nothing.
  3. He behaves as if, he were psycho.
  4. He acted as though, he had seen nothing.

6. So that/in order that(যাতে).

str:
Present: ...so that/in order that + Sub + can + V1 + ext

Past: ...so that/in order that + Sub + could + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. Niva studied regularly so that she could get A+.
  2. Nijhum neads newpaper in order that she can enrich her knowledge.

7. in order to(যাতে/ উদ্দেশ্য).

str: ...in order to + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. He worked hard in order to earn money.
  2. We have to learn English in order to get a good job.

Rule-8:

str: ... + with a view to/ look forward to/ Come forward to/ with an eye to + v + ing + ext

Examples:
  1. We go to abroad with a view to travelling.
  2. when anyone falls in danger, other come forward to helping him.
  3. We all should work together with a view to solving any every problem.

Rule-9:

str:

  • It is high time + Sub + V2 + ext
  • It was high time + Sub + had + V3 + ext
  • It is time + to + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. It is high time, we changed our bad habit.
  2. Corruption hindens the development of a nation. It is high time, we stopped it.
  3. It is time to sleep.

Rule-10:

str: ...So(এত) + adj/adv + that(যে) + Sub + can/could + not + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. The weather was so hot that we could not go to play.
  2. He runs so slowly that he can not win the Race.
  3. We all should work together with a view to solving any every problem.

Rule-11:

str: ...Too(এত) + adj + to(যে) + Sub + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. The weather is was too hot to go to school.
  2. He was too weak to work.

Rule-12: since যদি মাঝে থাকেঃ

str:

  • Present + since(adv of time) + Sub + V2 + ext
  • Past + since(adv of time) + Sub + had + V3 + ext

Examples:
  1. 10 years have passed since my grandfather died.
  2. 10 years passed since I had met him last.

Rule-13: since যদি শুরুতে থাকেঃ

str: Since/As(কারণ/ যেহেতু) + Sub + verb + ext + , Sub + verb + ext

Examples:
  1. As Tarin was ill, she could not go to school.
  2. Since Urboshi reads well, she gets A+.

Rule-14: Used to

str:

  • অতীতে কোন কাজ নিয়মিত করতোঃ Sub + used to + V1 + ext
  • অভ্যস্ত Sub + be verb/get + used to + v + ing + ext

Examples:
  1. We used to catch fishes in the river everyday.
  2. He has (got/been) used to speaking English.
  3. He is used to singing songs.

Rule-15: Double Comperative(যত..তত)**

str: The + adj + Sub + verb + ext + , + the + adj2 + Sub + veb + ext

Examples:
  1. The more you will read, the more you will learn
  2. The more beautiful she is, the uglier her mind is.
  3. The faster Jenifa runs, the sooner she reaches

Rule-16: Relative Pronoun**

str: The + noun + clause marker(Subঅনুযায়ী) + 2nd sen + 1st sen

1st sentence: verb + ext

2nd sentence:
clause marker যদি Subএর কাজ না করেঃ Sub + verb + ext
clause marker যদি Subএর কাজ করেঃverb + ext

Examples:
  1. The man who works hard, can earn money.
  2. The man who is lazy, can not shine in life.
  3. The math that, you solved, was wrong.
  4. The place where we are born, is our motherland.

Rule-17:

str: শুরুতে Clause marker যুক্ত বাক্যের structure same হয়

Examples:
  1. When Jerin was a child, she could not walk.
  2. As B.D. is an agricultural country, most of the people are farmers.

Rule-18: Though/ Although(যদিও)

str: Though/ Although + (Sub + verb + ext),-|- (Sub + verb + ext).

Examples:
  1. Though Jerin studies regularly, she gets poor marks
  2. Though B.D. is a small country, it has vast population.
  3. Many people go smoking though, it is harmful for health.

Rule-19: Inspite of/ Despite(সত্তেও)

str: Inspite of/ Despite + (v + ing)/ noun + ext, -|- (Sub + verb + ext).

Examples:
  1. Despite his inocence, teacher punished him.
  2. Inspite of working hand, he failed.

Rule-20: After

str:

  • Sub + V2 + ext + after + Sub + had + V3 + ext
  • Sub + will + V1 + ext + afetr + Sub + have/ has + V3 + ext

Examples:
  1. You will go to school after you have taken your Breakfast.
  2. After they have sold their house, they will go to USA.
  3. After Messi had joined the team, his team lost everymatch.*****

Rule-21: যদি অর্থ প্রকাশ করে।

str: Sub + will + verb + ext + (provided/ provided that/ providing that) + present tense

Examples:
  1. we will play provided the weather is fine
  2. We will get bumper crops provided that it rains properly.

Rule-22: সম্ভাবনা

str: Sub + will + verb + ext + incase + present tense or,
same structure + incase + same structure

Examples:
  1. I carried an umbrella incase it rained
  2. I will help you incase you help me.

Rule-23: or/ otherwise

str: Imperative sentence + or/ otherwise + Sub + will + V1 + ext

Examples:
  1. Read attentively. Otherwise, you will fail
  2. Be united. Or,you will fall in danger.

Rule-24:

str: When + Sub + has/ have + V3 + ext + , + Sub + will + V1 + ext.

Examples:
  1. When you have finished your work, you will go home

Rule-25:

str: Sub + verb + ext + but/never the less/while/where as/ on the other hand/on the contrary + Sub + verb + ext.

Examples:
  1. Sumve is hard working where as, his brother is lazy.
  2. He is dishonest yet, everybody loves him.

str:
বাক্যের ক্ষেত্রেঃ দুপাশের structure হবে same. Parts of speech এর ক্ষেত্রেও same part of speech.

Examples:
  1. Sumve not only gets A+ but also get scholarship.
  2. Protik is not only cleaver but also
